Tic Tac Toe

Created in early 2020.

Similar to the Blackjack game, I had to create a tic tac toe script that would allow two people to play on the computer. The program creates a board with positions from 1-9 and the players alternate between choosing where to make a play. The game will detect when a player wins and ask if they wish to play again or end the session. The output below shows the first move of a new game, in which a player decided to put an “X” on the first position.
